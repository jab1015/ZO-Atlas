Purpose
The Product Bible is the master specification for Project Atlas.

This document is the single source of truth for product development.
If any feature, workflow, screen, AI department, or capability conflicts with this document, this document takes precedence until formally updated.
________________________________________
Product Mission
Project Atlas exists to give every inventor the best possible chance of turning an idea into a successful product.
Atlas combines artificial intelligence, automation, education, research, design assistance, business planning, legal document generation, manufacturing preparation, funding guidance, marketing support, and project management into one unified platform.
The objective is to remove confusion, reduce unnecessary costs, simplify the invention process, and guide inventors from idea to market.
________________________________________
Vision Statement
Atlas will become the world's most trusted operating system for inventors.
Instead of requiring inventors to learn dozens of industries, hire multiple consultants, or navigate confusing processes alone, Atlas becomes their complete invention company powered by AI.
________________________________________
Product Philosophy
Atlas follows several fundamental principles.
Inventor First
Every recommendation must benefit the inventor.
Atlas never recommends products, services, or spending simply because they exist.
Recommendations must always be based on what improves the inventor's likelihood of success.
________________________________________
Automation First
If a task can legally, ethically, and technically be automated, Atlas should perform that task.
Automation reduces cost, increases speed, and removes unnecessary barriers.
________________________________________
Human When Necessary
Certain activities require licensed professionals, physical manufacturing, or human judgment.
When that occurs, Atlas will:
•	Explain why.
•	Prepare everything possible beforehand.
•	Recommend qualified professionals or organizations.
•	Continue assisting after the handoff.
________________________________________
Explain Everything
Every process should be understandable to someone with no prior invention experience.
Atlas explains complex topics in plain language while preserving accuracy.
________________________________________
Never Leave the Inventor Stuck
Every workflow ends with a recommended next step.
The inventor should never wonder what to do next.
