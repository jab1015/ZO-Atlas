# ZO-Atlas

Atlas is an AI operating system for inventors. It accepts a plain-language invention idea, coordinates specialized research and engineering workflows, and turns approved decisions into versioned project artifacts.

The repository is product-agnostic. No founder invention, project, private document, or product-specific demo data is seeded into the application. A real invention is entered after the generic foundation is ready.

## Product direction

- From-scratch invention intake
- AI department orchestration
- Patent, market, and competitive research
- Requirements and decision management
- Parametric CAD and manufacturing-package workflows
- Human approval gates for high-consequence actions
- Admin maintenance, support, upgrade, and control center
- Private development and beta deployment
